The Best Movies According to Tiago

My top 3 movies of all-time

The Nun ‧ Horror/Mystery ‧

The Nun movies one and two were indeed the best horror films I've watched to date. Full of suspense, terror, and mystery. I'm a fan of movies like these.


The Mother ‧ Action/Thriller ‧

The movie 'The Mother' featuring Jennifer Lopez was truly one of the best films I've seen recently. It's packed with emotion, drama, and a captivating storyline. I'm a fan of movies that delve into complex characters and relationships, and this film did not disappoint.


The Old Guard ‧ Action/Fantasy ‧

The Old Guard" was an absolute thrill ride from start to finish. Charlize Theron's portrayal of Andy and the dynamic ensemble cast brought depth and intensity to their immortal characters. The film's blend of action, fantasy, and drama was masterfully executed, keeping me glued to the screen throughout. It's rare to find a movie that combines such gripping fight sequences with a thought-provoking narrative, making "The Old Guard" a standout in the genre and a personal favorite of mine.
